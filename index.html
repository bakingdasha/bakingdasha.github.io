Darya's Little World

Baking is an art and photography is a means to capture that art as an evergreen memory, the one who combines them both is none but your very own Dasha!

Welcome to my baking blog, Darya's Little World! This is my little corner of the internet where I get to share my lifelong love of baking with you. Ever since I was a little girl I've been enchanted by the magical art of baking.

There's something so satisfying about mixing up a bowl of cookie dough or kneading just the right amount of flour into bread dough or simply just listening to the sound of bread baking! I love watching flat cake batters transform into tall, fluffy layers and simpler ingredients become complex, decadent desserts.

Baking is equal parts science and creativity - my favorite combination! While crucial techniques like proper mixing, leavening, and temperature control are important, I also love letting my imagination run wild with unique flavor combinations and decorative designs. Finding the perfect balance between following recipes exactly and exercising my personal baking style is highly rewarding.

My goal with Darya's Little World is to share glimpses of my affairs with baking axaxa.

Expert in-

  • Bread baking - sourdough, baguettes, whole wheat, rye, etc.
  • Pastry - croissants, danishes, tarts, galettes, etc.
  • Cake decorating - buttercreams, fondant, royal icing, etc.
  • Cookie and bar desserts - chocolate chip, oatmeal, lemon bars, etc.
  • Seasonal and holiday treats - pies, gingerbread, fruit cakes
