About Darya

Darya Tretyakova is a professional baker from Yekaterinburg, Russia. She has been baking for the last 4 years and loves creating delicious baked goods. Darya finds joy in taking simple ingredients and transforming them into beautiful and tasty treats through the artistic process of baking.

When she is not in the kitchen, Darya enjoys photography. She likes to capture the beauty around her through her lens. Photographing foods, landscapes, and people allows Darya to hone her creative skills.

With her passion for baking and eye for photography, Darya is dedicated to her crafts. She hopes to continue perfecting her skills and bringing joy to others through her creations.
